ABOUT VALORANT

Valorant is a 5v5 multiplayer first-person shooter (FPS) where one team attacks and the other defends. The main game mode, Search and Destroy, is very similar to CS:GO. The attacking team's goal is to plant a bomb (called a spike) and have it detonate, while the defending team tries to avoid the bomb by defusing it in condition with the timer. Regardless of whether the spike is planted or not, if a squad is wiped out before any other victory condition is met, the opposing squad will win.

CHARACTERS

Characters are arranged into class. There are four classes of characters in Valorant.

DUELIST: Primary attackers that are cable of pushing the enemies. Either one or many enemies, duelist roll is to fight anyone no matter what.

INITIATOR: Initiates during a push on site or enemies. They have a skill sets containing flash, and recon abilities. Initiator provides the team a advantage in clearing/pushing into areas and intel on where enemies could be.

CONTROLLER: Controllers main roles is to hinder the opposing team, by using their abilities. They block of certain areas, that help clearing angles easier. They can also block entries, making it harder for enemies to rush.

SENTINEL: Defenders of a site. Main goal of sentinels is to hold down one site with their abilities. With their abilities being strong, it makes enemies hesitate to switch and push the other site.

ESPORTS

Riot Games has developed an esports ecosystem for Valorant, including regional leagues, international tournaments, and a structured competitive ladder. Some of the major Valorant esports events include the Valorant Champions Tour (VCT), which features various stages leading up to a global championship, as well as regional leagues like the Valorant Champions Tour North America (VCT NA), Valorant Champions Tour Europe (VCT EU), and others.

Valorant esports refers to competitive gaming events and tournaments played in Riot Games' tactical first-person shooter game, Valorant. Esports in Valorant involve professional teams competing against each other in organized leagues and tournaments for cash prizes, recognition, and glory. Players form teams and compete in matches that require strategic thinking, teamwork, and precise aiming skills.
